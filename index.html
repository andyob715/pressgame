Lock or Press
After each reveal, you make the call.
Lock ✓
Press →
Lock — bank your word, take your bonus, and you're done. Round resolves once the AI plays out. Lock early for a bonus: +2 at Flop, +1 at Turn.
Press — reveal the next letter and hunt for something longer. If you have a word typed, it's saved as your safety net. But if you end up with a shorter word than you started with, you lose 3pts.

💡 Pro tip: Type your safe word first — then decide whether to press. If time runs out, your typed word auto-locks.
